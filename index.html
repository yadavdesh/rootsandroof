Why Roots and Roof

A guide who has walked the path you're on

Buying property in a foreign country is equal parts exciting and disorienting — new laws, new language, a market that plays by its own rules. Roots and Roof was founded to close that gap: a single trusted Berlin property advisor who understands both where you come from and how Berlin works, so you can make confident decisions instead of guesses.

Desh has spent over a decade between Berlin and the international expat community, helping families and investors turn 'someday' into a signed contract. She built Roots and Roof around a simple idea — that finding a home abroad shouldn't mean navigating it alone.

  • Fluent guidance in English, Hindi, German and Russian
  • Direct network of notaries, banks and surveyors
  • End-to-end support, from search to keys in hand
